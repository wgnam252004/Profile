University

Posts and Telecommunications Institute of Technology

Posts and Telecommunications Institute of Technology (PTIT) is a university under the Ministry of Information and Communications, specializing in training in information technology, telecommunications electronics, information security, business administration and marketing. The main campus of the academy is located in Hanoi, and there is also a campus in Ho Chi Minh City. PTIT stands out with its practical training programs, modern learning environment and close cooperation with large technology enterprises. The school also has strengths in scientific research and technology transfer in the fields of telecommunications and IT.
